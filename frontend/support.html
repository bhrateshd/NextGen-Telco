Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my new phone?

You can activate your new phone through our website, mobile app, or by calling customer service. You'll need your device IMEI number and account information.

What is your coverage area?

NextGen Telco provides nationwide 5G and LTE coverage. Check our coverage map on the website to see coverage in your area.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime through your account dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What are the contract terms?

NextGen Telco offers no contract plans. You can cancel or modify your service anytime without penalties.

How do I check my data usage?

Log into your account dashboard to view real-time data usage. You can also download the NextGen Telco app for usage tracking.

Do you offer family plans?

Yes! We offer flexible family plans for 2-5 lines with significant savings. Visit our Plans page to see family plan options.
